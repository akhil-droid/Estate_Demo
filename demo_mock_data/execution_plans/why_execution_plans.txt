Execution Plans Summary → For Orchestrator's brain

Query arrives → Orchestrator looks up which plan_id matches

Execution Steps Detailed → For Orchestrator's execution

Tells exact sequence: Step 1 → Step 2 → Step 3
parallel_group: A, A, B means first two run together, B waits
dependencies: Step 5 needs Step 2 done first
assigned_agent: Who does what
